Cornell Tech: Women in Entrepreneurship and Technology New York

Developer Mentor (2017)

I served as a mentor for a team of students interested in learning more about the technical development process. Part of the experience was a 2-day product sprint, where teams were required to build out a civic product that would help senior centers on Roosevelt Island operate more efficiently. My team ideated a platform where seniors could easily submit feedback, register for classes, contact their officials, and view events. During the sprint, I taught students the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git. Final presentations were hosted by Bloomberg NY.

Panelist (2016, 2017)

I served as a panelist for students to discuss civic technology, data science, and the work being done in both spaces by Microsoft NY.
